Chili Recipe

The Best Classic Chili -

This classic chili is perfect for a weeknight meal because it can be made in minutes. The only thing to chop is an onion.
This traditional chili recipe is just like mom used to make with ground beef, beans, and a simple homemade blend of chili seasonings.

I’ve been playing around with this chili recipe for the past few months, making it many times.
My family didn’t mind the repetition at all because I really do believe that I ended up with the best chili ever!
