Banana Bread Recipe

Description

I made this banana bread recipe when I had over ripe bananas and left-over buttermilk in the refrigerator that I didn’t know what to do with.
I was so happy with the result. This is now my go-to banana recipe. It’s that good!

This bread is great as soon as it’s cool enough to eat. But, it’s even better if you can cover it and let it set in the refrigerator for a few hours.
I like to make it in the evening and serve it for breakfast the next day. Letting the bread rest in the refrigerator makes it incredibly moist. And,
it stays moist for days.


Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 medium overripe bananas, mashed with a fork (about 1 cup)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup canola oil (or your preferred cooking oil)
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
